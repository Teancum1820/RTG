Join us on a remarkable journey through the sacred sites of the Restoration with renowned author and speaker Brad Wilcox.

About Brad Wilcox

Bradley Ray Wilcox is a dedicated educator, author, and religious leader. He serves as a professor of Ancient Scripture at Brigham Young University (BYU) and, from April 2020 until August 2025, served in the Young Men General Presidency of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, culminating in his role as First Counselor starting in April 2023.

Raised in Provo and briefly living in Ethiopia during his youth, Brad served as a missionary in the Chile Viña del Mar Mission, where he also wrote the mission song. He completed his bachelor’s degree in elementary education at BYU in 1985, later earning a master’s degree in teaching and learning from the same institution, and a Ph.D. in curriculum and instruction (specializing in literacy) from the University of Wyoming.

At BYU, Brad has taught first in the Teacher Education Department and now in the Department of Ancient Scripture. He is also a prolific author and speaker, well known for his devotional talk, His Grace is Sufficient, which remains one of the most-watched BYU talks ever.

In his church service, Brad has held numerous leadership roles: bishop, high councilor, stake Young Men president, counselor in a stake presidency, member of the Sunday School General Board, and president of the Chile Santiago East Mission (2003–2006).

Brad is also an accomplished author, with works including The Continuous Atonement, The Best-Kept Secrets of Parenting, Raising Ourselves to the Bar, and many others. He has contributed to a wide range of magazines and journals.

He is married to Deborah G. Gunnell, a registered nurse and former missionary in Guatemala, and they are parents to four children.

FAQ:

What if my flight is delayed?

If there are changes to your flight coming into Buffalo, you will be required to pay for a taxi to meet us along the tour.

How much walking and stairs will there be?

This tour will have several short walks (less than a quarter mile) throughout each day. Optional longer walks (hills or up to half a mile) are available at some locations.

The Kirtland Temple is not handicap accessible. The main floor is a few steps up. To visit the 2nd and 3rd floors requires climbing several steep stairs.

The Garfield Monument is also only accessible by climbing about 20 stairs to the main level, and then several steep and winding steps to the mezzanine and balcony. There are other things to see nearby if you choose not to enter the monument.

What should I bring?

A small bag, purse, or backpack holds items you may need throughout the day. When you leave the hotel in the morning, you will not return until after dinner. Please plan accordingly. The bus will be secure during our stops for anything left on board.

The weather is notoriously fickle in Midwest. A rain jacket and sweater are good ideas. Umbrellas will be provided at certain sites as needed.

Though we will provide light snacks and water on the bus, you may want to bring snacks that fit your taste and preference, as well as a water bottle or other drink.

Please dress for comfort and mobility. There will not be any strenuous walking, but some ground may be uneven and there are several opportunities to climb stairs.

Seating will be available at many sites we visit. We will have folding camp chairs available at certain other locations for those who need them.

Will I be given a guidebook?

A full-color guidebook will be given to each guest on our tour, but you may wish to have a journal for note-taking. We will be referencing the Standard Works of scriptures of the restoration including the Holy Bible, Book of Mormon, and Doctrine & Covenants. Paper or digital copies will come in handy.

I need to cancel my trip. What is the refund policy?

We are sorry that you won’t be able to join us as planned. If you’ve paid in full for your trip, and we are able to replace your spot with a new guest, you will be refunded the cost of the trip, minus the $500 deposit. If we are unable to find another guest to fill your spot, we will be happy to book you on another tour or refund you 50% of your total amount paid.
